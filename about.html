About Us

EcoTrace is dedicated to helping individuals reduce their carbon footprints through educational and interactive tools on our website. We provide comprehensive information on carbon footprints, including their significance and sources like transportation and energy consumption. Our interactive quiz and carbon footprint calculator help users understand and estimate their personal carbon emissions. Additionally, we offer visual representations of global CO2 emissions and practical strategies for reducing carbon footprints. By providing personalized insights, EcoTrace empowers users to make environmentally conscious decisions and contribute to a greener planet.
